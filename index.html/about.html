About Monterrey Janitorial

Our mission

At Monterrey Janitorial, we are dedicated to delivering excellent cleaning services tailored to the unique needs of our clients. With a decade of commitment to cleanliness,we have become
a trusted team for hospitals, clinics, schools, and various health organizations.
Our mission is simple-to create and maintain pristine enviroments that contribute to the well-being of our community.

Founders & Visionaries

Founded 10 years ago by Rocio Mendez, Monterrey Janitorial is the result of a vision to elevate the standards of janitorial services in healthcare and educational settings.
With my extensive experience as a janitorial manager, and Martin F, a seasoned professional in the industry, we joined forces to establish a company that prioritizes excellence, reliability,
and client satisfaction.

Our Approach

Monterrey Janitorial believes in a client-centric approach, where each cleaning solution is customized to meet the specific requirements of our diverse clientele. We understand the
importance of maintaining hygienic enviroments in healthcare and education institutions, and our team is committed to upholding the highest standards.
From dusting and mopping to specialized services such as floor scrubbing and carpet cleaning, we take
pride in the thoroughness of our work.
