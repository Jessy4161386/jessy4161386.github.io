Jessica Toledo's Story

Hi everbody! My name is Jessica Toledo, and I am the website developer for the company Monterrey Janitorial. I had developed this website as a project for my web development class. I am a college studying currently pursuing my associates in information technology. My future plans also include studying for the CompTIA A+ exam, as I'm passionate about computers and the vast world of the internet. My journey in the world of technology begins with an interest in computer user support, and my goal is to establish a rewarding career as a computer user support specialist, gradually advancing in the field.

Why this project?

I took on the challenge of developing Monterrey Janitorial's website to promote the business's online presence. With a decade of growth behind it, this platform serves as a means to showcase our services to potential clients and organizations seeking cleaning services.

Development Process

Embarking on the creation of this website for Monterrey Janitorial was a fulfilling and creative experience. As the website developer, I poured my passion for technology and developed this website over time. This allowed me to showcase the companys growth and commitment to medical cleanliness over the past decade. The journey involved getting to work with HTML despite its complexities. The web development has been a rewarding experience, and I'm proud to contribute to Monterrey Janitorial.
