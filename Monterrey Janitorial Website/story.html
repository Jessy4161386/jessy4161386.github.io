Founders Story

Monterrey Janitorial emerged from the vision of Rocio Mendez, a dedicated professional with over a decade of experience in the cleaning industry.
Initially working as a subcontractor for esteemed institutions such as Lab Corporation, Banner Health, and Healthpoint solutions, Rocio embarked on her journey from the ground up.

Her commitment took root during her early days, handing various cleaning assignments in Central Downtown Phoenix. With meticulous attention to detail,
she honed her cleaning skills, ensuring every crook and cranny was throughouly cleaned. This hands-on experience served as the stepping stone
for Rocio to climb the professional ladder. Her dedication not only showcased her proficiency, but also brought forth a creative touch that significantly
contributed to the success of the businesses she served.

Her work has been praised by her supervisors, complimenting her creativty, with a specification to problem solving in a short time manner.
